About the Observatory

The Brazilian Extremist Content Hashing Observatory (BR-ECHO) is an academic, non-commercial research project developed at the University of São Paulo (USP) in collaboration with King’s College London (KCL).

Managed by researcher Ricardo Cabral Penteado, BR-ECHO utilizes advanced computational linguistics and hashing techniques to monitor and analyze public discourse trends.

Our work is strictly dedicated to scholarly research, aiming to contribute to the global understanding of digital platform governance and online safety through the TikTok Research API.
