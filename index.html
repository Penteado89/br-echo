BR-ECHO Research Project

About the Project

BR-ECHO is a non-commercial academic research initiative focused on the computational processing of natural language. Developed as part of doctoral research, the project analyzes public data to understand linguistic patterns and digital discourse.

This tool accesses the TikTok Research API strictly for scholarly analysis, adhering to all platform guidelines and data protection regulations.
