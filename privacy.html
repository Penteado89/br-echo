Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Data Collection

We only collect publicly available data provided by the TikTok Research API (such as video descriptions, view counts, and public metadata). We do not access private accounts, private messages, or restricted user information.

2. Use of Data

Data is used exclusively for academic linguistic analysis. We do not perform individual tracking or re-identification of users.

3. Data Retention & Security

All retrieved data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized researchers. We commit to deleting raw data once the specific research objective is achieved or upon expiration of the API access period, in compliance with GDPR (UK) and LGPD (Brazil) standards.

4. No Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share raw data with third parties or commercial entities.
