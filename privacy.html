Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the BR-ECHO Research Project, managed by Ricardo Cabral Penteado, handles data. This project is strictly for academic research conducted at the University of São Paulo (USP) in collaboration with King’s College London (KCL).

3. Data Storage and Access Security

All retrieved data is stored in secure environments. Access to the raw data and the BR-ECHO analysis dashboard is exclusively granted to the internal research team. We implement strict authentication measures to ensure that no unauthorized parties can access the research environment.

2. Purpose of Processing

Data is used solely for large-scale computational linguistic analysis to investigate social trends and public discourse patterns in the Brazilian context. No data is used for commercial, advertising, or profiling purposes.

3. Data Storage, Security, and Deletion

In compliance with TikTok Research Terms of Service, we do not build long-term databases of individual user activity. All research outputs are aggregated (e.g., statistical summaries and topic models). We implement technical measures to ensure that transient data is handled securely. All raw data retrieved via the API is deleted once the specific analysis for the academic paper or thesis chapter is completed.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any raw data with third parties. Findings are shared only as anonymized academic results in peer-reviewed publications and doctoral theses.

5. Compliance

This project adheres to the TikTok Developer Terms of Service and Research API Guidelines.
